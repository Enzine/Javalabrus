How to play Silly Naked Furry Bellybutton Pets?

You can see the traits and the mood of your pet on your screen. They are in a box with the dark grey background colour.

The traits go worst with time and you need to keep your pet happy and well by clicking the buttons in the MENU.

You can save your progress with the SAVE button.

In case you need this the help of this file, click the HELP button at any time.

Click the EXIT Button or the cross at the top when you wish to exit. Remember to save before you go! 

Enjoy!
